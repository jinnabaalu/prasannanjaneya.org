---
title: "About Sri Prassananjaneya Swamy Devalayam"
---

Sri Prassananjaneya Swamy Devalayam is a revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman. The temple is known for its serene atmosphere, ancient traditions, and the divine blessings of Sri Anjaneya Swamy.

The temple has been a center of devotion and spiritual activities for generations. Devotees from across the region visit the temple to seek the blessings of Lord Hanuman, who is revered for his strength, devotion, and unwavering service to Lord Rama.

The temple conducts daily pujas, special rituals on auspicious days, and grand celebrations during festivals like Hanuman Jayanti and Ugadi. The temple committee works tirelessly to maintain the temple, organize events, and serve the community through Annadanam (free meals) and other charitable activities.
