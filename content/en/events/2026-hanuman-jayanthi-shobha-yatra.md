## 🚩 Sri Hanuman Dvijayotsavam — Narsingi

On April 2, 2026, Sri Hanuman Dvijayotsavam was celebrated with immense devotion and grandeur at Sri Prassananjaneya Swamy Devalayam, Narsingi.

## Event Schedule

- 🙏 **Morning** — Special puja with the head priest
- 🚩 **Afternoon** — Decoration of the temple premises with saffron flags
- 🎵 **Evening** — Grand Hanuman Shobha Yatra (rally) with DJ music
- 🎶 **Night** — Bhajan program by devotees in the sacred presence of Lord Anjaneya

## Shobha Yatra — A River of Devotion

The entire village of Narsingi came together for the grand procession. Children, youth, and elders — everyone joined the Hanuman Shobha Yatra, walking together with unwavering devotion, becoming one with the divine in a joyous and blissful atmosphere that made the yatra a resounding success.

The Shobha Yatra was a magnificent sight — adorned with saffron flags, vibrant flowers, and the powerful chanting of "Jai Hanuman" echoing through every lane. Devotees walked with steadfast faith, singing bhajans and keerthanalu, filling the air with spiritual energy.

The enthusiastic participation of children and youth was truly remarkable. Our young ones are the future torchbearers of our traditions — when we lead from the front, they learn the values of devotion and community. In today's times, moving forward with unity is every individual's responsibility.

Beyond this event, every Hindu brother and sister is humbly requested to participate wholeheartedly in every community celebration and cultural program.

## Gratitude

As the Shobha Yatra wound its way through the entire village, the whole community was immersed in devotional bliss. Every household welcomed the procession with reverence, offering prayers and seeking the blessings of Sri Anjaneya Swamy.

By the grace of Lord Hanuman, the event concluded with tremendous success. Heartfelt gratitude to all the devotees, the youth who volunteered their service, and the temple Bhakta Brundam for making this celebration truly memorable.

**🚩 Jai Sri Ram! Jai Hanuman! 🚩**
